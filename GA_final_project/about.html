About Film Hunter

Film Hunter makes it easy to find the best movie playing near you, right now.

Ratings

Film Hunter assumes you want to see a good movie, so it only returns movies critics rated 75% or higher on rotten tomatoes. Click on a movie title to check out the reviews in more detail.

Location

Right now, Film Hunter is city-centric. It uses your current location and returns results within a three-mile radius.

Timing

Film Hunter assumes you want to see a movie soon(ish).

"Can't Hardly Wait" shows you movies starting in the next half hour.

"Coffee and Cigarettes" gives you a little more time - it shows you movies starting between half an hour and an hour from now.

"Dinner With Andre" shows you movies starting between one and two hours from now.

Results always start 15 minutes before the current time. (Because "There Will Be Previews".)

Film Retriever

Film Retriever is Film Hunter's eager-to-please sibling. He doesn't judge your taste and will bring back movies with any minimum rating you specify. Who knows - maybe he just assumes you're looking for the next Plan 9 From Outer Space.
