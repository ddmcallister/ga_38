About Film Hunter

Film Hunter makes it easy to find the best movie playing near you, right now.

How Does It Work?

Ratings

Film Hunter assumes you want to see a good movie, so it only returns movies critics rated 80% or higher on rotten tomatoes. Click on the movie title to check out their ratings in more detail.

Location

Right now, Film Hunter is city-centic. It uses your current location and returns results within a one mile radius.

Timing

Film Hunter assumes you want to see a movie soon(ish).

"Can't Hardly Wait" shows you movies starting in the next half hour.

"Coffee and Cigarettes" gives you a little more time - it shows you movies starting in the next hour.

"Dinner With Andre" shows you starting at most two hours from now.

And we always start our results 15 minutes before the current time. (If this category had a button, it would be called "There Will Be Previews".)
