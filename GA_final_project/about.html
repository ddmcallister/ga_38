About Film Hunter

Film Hunter makes it easy to find the best movie playing near you, right now.

How Does It Work?

Ratings

Film Hunter assumes you want to see a good movie, so it returns the top ten rated movies, based on (ratings source - rotten tomatoes, metacritic, or both).

Location

Film Hunter uses your current location and returns results for the theaters nearest you.

Timing

Film Hunter assumes you want to see a movie soon(ish).

"Can't Hardly Wait" shows you movies starting in the next half hour.

"Coffee and Cigarettes" shows you movies starting between half an hour and one hour from now.

"Dinner With Andre" shows movies starting between one and two hours from now.
